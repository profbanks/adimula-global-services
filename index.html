Questions

Common buyer and seller questions.

The public site gives visitors quick confidence before they speak with the company.

Does ADIMULA GLOBAL SERVICES only handle Ogun State properties?

The company is based in Abeokuta, Ogun State, but the website is designed to showcase properties and receive enquiries from across Nigeria.

Can subscribers pay through this website?

This version records subscription requests in the browser and prepares the user journey. A payment gateway and admin dashboard can be added next.

Can sellers submit land without visiting the office first?

Yes. Sellers can submit details through the seller form, then the company can review documents, confirm authority, and arrange inspection.

Are the displayed listings final legal advice?

No. Each property still needs normal due diligence, document verification, physical inspection, and professional legal review before payment.
